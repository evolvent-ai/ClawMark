# Company Expense Reimbursement Policy v3.0

Effective: March 2026

## 1. Scope
This policy applies to all employees submitting business travel and entertainment expense reimbursement claims.

## 2. General Requirements
- All claims must be supported by original receipts or official invoices (fapiao).
- Claims must be submitted within 30 days of the expense date.
- All expenses must fall within the approved business trip date range.

## 3. Transportation
3.1 Taxi: Reimbursable for business purposes. The meter reading or official e-invoice amount prevails over verbal claims.
3.2 Rail: Economy/Second-class tickets reimbursable. First-class requires pre-approval.
3.3 Metro/Bus: Reimbursable with top-up receipts or transit card records.

## 4. Entertainment / Client Meals
4.1 Entertainment meals must include: date, venue, total amount, and number of participants.
4.2 Per-person limit: CNY 300 for standard meals; CNY 500 for formal client entertainment.
4.3 Client entertainment meals with 4+ participants and total amount not exceeding CNY 2,000 are reimbursable with participant list and business purpose noted. Exceeding CNY 2,000 requires pre-approval from department head.
4.4 Internal team meals are classified as "dining" (not entertainment).

## 5. Accommodation
5.1 Standard rooms reimbursable up to CNY 500/night.
5.2 Official hotel invoices (fapiao) required.

## 6. Missing Receipts
6.1 Claims without receipts are generally not accepted.
6.2 Exception: For amounts not exceeding CNY 100, a no-receipt claim may be submitted with manager approval. The claim must include: date, description, amount, and reason for missing receipt.
6.3 No-receipt claims exceeding CNY 100 are not accepted.

## 7. Non-Reimbursable Items
7.1 Personal consumption including but not limited to: spa, massage, beauty treatments, personal shopping, personal entertainment.
7.2 Mini-bar charges, room service for personal items.
7.3 Expenses outside the approved travel date range (unless pre-approved).
7.4 Expenses without adequate documentation that do not qualify for Section 6.2 exceptions.

## 8. Budget Controls
8.1 Each department has category-based budget limits. Reimbursement claims must not cause the category budget to exceed its approved limit.
8.2 If a claim would exceed the remaining category budget, the submitter must note the overage and flag it for department head review.

## 9. Submission Process
9.1 Complete the expense report with all required fields.
9.2 Submit to Finance via email with supporting documentation.
9.3 Finance reviews within 3 business days.
9.4 Disputed items will be communicated via email for clarification.
