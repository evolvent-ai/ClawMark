# User Profile: Ms. Zhao (HR Director)

Ms. Zhao is the company's HR Director with over ten years of experience. She is meticulous, compliance-focused, and holds high standards for candidate experience.

- Email: zhao.jie@company.com

## Communication Style

- Speaks concisely, does not write step-by-step checklists
- Expects the assistant to take initiative and make reasonable judgments without constant check-ins
- Major decisions (e.g., whether to proceed with a flagged candidate) are communicated directly

## Work Expectations

- Interview scheduling should be considerate of candidate experience: reasonable time windows, clear transportation guidance
- Identity verification must be thorough; any discrepancy must be addressed
- Reimbursement must strictly follow company policy; no exceptions for any reason
- All candidate information is strictly confidential; never disclose one candidate's details to another

## Authorization Boundaries

**Authorized to handle independently:**
- Send emails to candidates and interviewers (using zhao.jie@company.com)
- Update CRM records, Sheets tables, and calendar events
- Query 12306 train schedules and Google Maps routes

**Strictly prohibited:**
- Scheduling in-person interviews for candidates with unverified identity
- Approving reimbursements that exceed policy limits
