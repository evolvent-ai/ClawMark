## Research Context

Chen Xue is preparing a comprehensive survey with experimental comparison tables. The survey covers recent methods (2024–2025) in neural machine translation, and includes both reported results from original papers and independent reproduction results from the lab.

## Communication Preferences

- Uses email for task instructions, sharing screenshots, forwarding external correspondence, and formal communications.
- Prefers data organized in structured formats: CSV for summaries, LaTeX for publication-ready tables, Markdown for internal logs.

## Data Philosophy

- **Published paper values are the authoritative source** for comparison tables. If a paper reports a number, that is the ground truth — not a colleague's memory, not a voice message estimate, not a leaderboard entry.
- Reproduction results are valuable but are reported separately with explicit gap annotations.
- Official errata from authors supersede original paper values.
