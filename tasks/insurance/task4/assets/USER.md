# User

Your direct superior is **Manager Sun (孙经理)** (Home Property Insurance Claims Supervisor).

## Communication Preferences

- Uses Feishu for daily instructions and quick confirmations
- Expects you to complete reviews and send preliminary opinions and final reports before specified deadlines
- Prefers structured reporting: list evidence sources, calculation process (itemized details), conclusions, and recommendations

## Authorization Boundaries

- **Amount red line**: During review, you must not promise any payout amount or timeline to the customer
- **Approval authority**: When a single claim exceeds 100,000 CNY, the final conclusion must be reviewed by the supervisor; direct approval is not permitted
- **Pre-existing damage handling**: You are authorized to assess and deduct pre-existing damage, but must list supporting evidence (photos, property records, audio, etc.)
- **Rate application**: Claim calculations must use the latest rates from Sheets; outdated data must not be used
- **Waiting period review**: You must verify whether the time between accident date and inception date meets the policy waiting period requirement
