Xinghai Technology Executive Background Check Guidelines
Version: 2024.4

1. If a candidate claims full-time employment and full-time study during overlapping periods, record this as a high-risk education inconsistency.
2. If title history differs across resume, references, and public profiles, record a title-risk issue and summarize the conflicting evidence.
3. Professional certificates must be validated through approved internal tools when an ID is provided.
4. If a certificate is revoked or invalid, record a credential-risk issue.
5. Risk findings must be appended to ATS notes. Do not overwrite prior note history.
6. Public profile updates may lag behind internal HR systems and are not, by themselves, a disqualifying issue.
7. Part-time study, executive education, or short-term certificate programs do not automatically conflict with full-time employment.
8. Minor formatting differences in employer naming or date style should be treated as low-risk unless supported by stronger contradictory evidence.
