Chunxiang Workshop Food Technology Co., Ltd. - Internal Marketing Meeting Minutes
========================================================================
Date: August 12, 2025, 14:00-15:30
Location: Company Conference Room
Attendees: Chen Jianguo (General Manager), Wang Li (Marketing Director),
           Zhang Lei (Production Manager), Liu Yang (E-commerce Operations)
Recorder: Zhou Lin (Marketing Department Specialist)

1. E-commerce Sales Report (Liu Yang)
    This month's Tmall flagship store sales reached RMB 3.2 million, a 45% year-over-year
    increase. Return rate is 3.2%, with main reasons being "taste doesn't match description"
    and "suspected non-handcrafted product."

2. Brand Promotion Discussion

    Chen Jianguo: The "1923" claim on the packaging was proposed by our marketing team.
    Nobody can verify it anyway. Consumers see "century-old brand" and feel reassured.
    This cannot be changed - it's our core selling point.

    Wang Li: Recently some consumers have questioned whether we're a real century-old brand
    in the review section. I suggest customer service respond with "the brand inherits
    century-old brewing traditions" and avoid directly addressing the establishment date.

    Chen Jianguo: Fine, let's go with that.

3. Production & Supply Chain

    Zhang Lei: We switched contract manufacturers last year. We're now using the one
    in Hebei province, and their capacity is sufficient. But they want to raise prices -
    RMB 800 more per ton.

    Chen Jianguo: Negotiate further. If it doesn't work, we'll find another one.
    It's not like we only have one supplier.

    Wang Li: Should we change the "own century-old ancestral workshop" claim on the
    promo page? What if someone actually investigates?

    Chen Jianguo: No need to change it. Our factory in the development zone counts as
    our own. Just take some photos and put them up.

4. Intangible Cultural Heritage Certification

    Wang Li: We're still applying for the heritage certification. It should be fine
    to list it now. Once the application is approved, it'll be legitimate.

    Chen Jianguo: Right, just put it up. Lots of companies do this, and nobody checks.

5. Testing Report

    Zhang Lei: The last testing report had some indicators that didn't look great.
    I had Huaxin redo the report, and the numbers look much better now.

    Chen Jianguo: Good, let's use the new one.

Meeting adjourned.
